Economist and Data Scientist

I currently work as a data engineer in the financial industry. Before my current job, I worked on quantitative models for market risk.

As a project, I have developed financialforecast, a Bayesian model that exploits the asyncronous releases of financial statements to make real-time predictions about company financials.

From September 2018 to February 2020, I was Assistant Professor of Economics at Stockholm School of Economics SITE. During my time as an academic, I was lucky to collaborate with brilliant co-authors on a few published research papers in the areas of development economics and political economy. I also developed a course in geospatial data analysis that I continue to teach.

I did my PhD at London Business School.
